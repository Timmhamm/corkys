About Climate Zones

Climate zones help gardeners and builders understand the typical weather patterns in their area. The IECC (International Energy Conservation Code) climate zones are used for building energy codes, while USDA hardiness zones help determine which plants will thrive in your location.

Use the location button to automatically detect your zone, or manually select your state and county from the dropdown menus above.
