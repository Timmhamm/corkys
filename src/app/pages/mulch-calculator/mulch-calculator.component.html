About Mulch Calculator

Use this calculator to determine how much mulch you need for your landscaping project. Enter your measurements in any unit (inches, feet, yards, centimeters, or meters), and the calculator will provide the volume in cubic yards, cubic feet, and cubic meters.

Recommended Depth: 2-3 inches for most garden beds, 3-4 inches for pathways and slopes.
