About Corky's Garden Path

Welcome to Corky's Garden Path

Corky's Garden Path is more than just a garden center – we're a community hub for gardening enthusiasts, home decor lovers, and anyone who appreciates the beauty of nature. Located in Scott Twp, Pennsylvania, we've been serving our community with quality plants, garden supplies, and home decor items for years.

Our mission is to help you create beautiful spaces both inside and outside your home. Whether you're looking for the perfect houseplant, planning your garden, or searching for unique home decor pieces, our knowledgeable staff is here to help.
